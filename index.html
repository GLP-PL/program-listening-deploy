Know when delivery is driftingbefore it shows up in a replan.

Program Listening quietly monitors pressure, misalignment, and delivery strain so leaders can act early – without changing how teams work.

Early warning for delivery drift in any business.

Most delivery failures are quiet at first.

Missed signals.

Small misalignments.

Decisions made slightly too late.

By the time problems appear in delivery delays, increased costs, or executive pressure, the damage is already baked in.

Program Listening exists to catch this earlier.

Listens continuously

Observes signals across delivery, systems, and teams.

Detects drift

Flags inconsistency, overload, and hidden strain as it emerges.

Surfaces early signals

Makes pressure visible before it becomes noise or crisis.

No dashboards to babysit. No consultants embedded full-time. Just early clarity.
