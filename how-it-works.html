How Program Listening Works

Program Listening is not a dashboard.

It is not a reporting tool.

It is an early warning system for delivery drift.

1

Configure

We configure Program Listening to reflect how your organisation currently operates.

  • What the organisation is focused on
  • Where attention is currently stretched
  • How decisions typically flow

No workshops. No reorganisation. No delivery changes.

2

Listen

The system listens for changes in how work is progressing – looking for patterns that indicate strain, misalignment, or drift.

It observes how delivery, systems, and teams behave – without interrupting them.

3

Surface

You receive:

  • Early warning signals
  • Plain-English summaries
  • Clear indicators of rising pressure

Program Listening does not tell you what to do – it tells you where attention is needed.

Program Listening observes continuously. It does not manage, intervene, or replace your teams.
